Still Not Receiving Messages?

For Twilio Trial Accounts: You need to verify your personal phone number first:

  1. Go to Twilio Console - Verified Numbers
  2. Click "Add a new number"
  3. Enter YOUR personal cell phone number (the same one you entered above)
  4. Verify it with the code Twilio sends
  5. Come back here and test again

For Paid Twilio Accounts: SMS should work immediately to any number without verification.
